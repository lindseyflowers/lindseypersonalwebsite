News Release: Upcoming Sustainability Week Reminds Blacksburg Citizens of Sustainable Options News releases inform journalists newsworthy stories happening within an organization. This news release campaigns for the annual Blacksburg Sustainability Week and mentions that locals can make efforts to live sustainable before the week by visiting their local farmers markets.

Opinion Editorial: Organic Produce: The Secret to Sustainability. Opinion editorials provide an editor's opinion on trending topics in the publication's particular field. This op-ed focuses on the GMO debate that has been occuring with health professionals and sustainability campaigns.

Feature Story: Virginia Tech and the Town of Blacksburg Take Strides Toward Sustainability. Feature stories are in-depth thoroughly researched articles. They provide personal narrative, and have a focus relevant to the publication's audience. This particular one focuses on Virginia Tech's sustainability initiatives and how Blacksburg locals can live with a local and sustainable mindset.

Playtime Project Blog Post: NASA Astronaut Touches Down at Playtime. This blog post summarizes the visit of Charles Bolden, former NASA astronaut, to the D.C. General Homeless Shelter in the nation's capital and the effect it had on the children.

Playtime Project Blog Post: Preteen Program Hosts Midsummer Gala.This blog post covers a small gala hosted by the non-profit that gave the children in the homeless shelter a chance to practice life skills, engage in imaginative play, and triumph over inequity.
